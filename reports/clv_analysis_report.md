Customer Lifetime Value Analysis
Project Overview

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis helps businesses understand the total revenue a customer generates throughout their relationship with the company.

This project analyzes transaction data from an online retail store to identify high-value customers, revenue trends, and purchasing behavior.

Dataset

Source: Online Retail Dataset

Columns used:

CustomerID
InvoiceNo
InvoiceDate
Quantity
UnitPrice
Country

Data Cleaning Steps

Removed cancelled transactions
Removed missing Customer IDs
Removed negative quantities
Created Revenue column

Revenue formula:
Revenue = Quantity × UnitPrice

Key Metrics

Total Revenue
Total sales generated by all customers.
Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
Estimated revenue generated by a customer over time.
Purchase Frequency
Average number of purchases per customer.
Average Order Value
Average spending per order.

Dashboard Insights

Revenue by Country
United Kingdom generates the highest revenue compared to other countries.

Top Customers
A small group of customers contribute a significant portion of total revenue.

Monthly Revenue Trend
Revenue increases steadily over time with seasonal fluctuations.

Customer Purchase Behav
Most customers make only a few purchases, while a small percentage are highly active buyers.

Customer Value Distribution
CLV distribution shows that high-value customers are limited but generate large revenue.

Business Recommendations

• Focus marketing campaigns on high-value customers
• Improve retention strategies for repeat buyers
• Expand marketing in high-revenue countries
• Implement loyalty programs to increase purchase frequency
